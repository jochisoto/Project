Helpful Sites

Tom's hardware is a website that everybody with a computer should have. Delivering unbiased technology news, articles, reviews and reports to IT professionals and tech enthusiasts since 1996.

Google(lol) is a search engine where you can find almost anything you are looking for. Not my favorite search engine but still very useful. Search the world's info, including webpages, images, videos and more.

Logical Increments is a website where you can find a very deep guide for every... EVERY computer part. It also has a guide to build computers and to buy computer accessories and TVs.

Top Ten Reviews is a website that can help you to find any kind of "top 10" hardware or apps. It is helpful if you want to compare different programs. it is the place to read insightful product reviews, software ratings, and electronics comparisons to stay informed.

Tom's Guide is a website that was made from the makers of Tom's Hardware. It was made to guide people that doesn't know a lot about computers and to compare different kinds of hardware and software.

Lifehacker is a weblog about life hacks and software which launched on January 31, 2005. The site is owned by Gawker Media. It is a site specialized on making your technological life much easier.
